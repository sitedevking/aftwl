Our Lovely Family

Gibril Mary

Gibril Mary lives with his father and grandparent in a remote village in the northern province of the country. He is a Themneh by tribe and they practice the Christian faith. The family dwells in a mud brick house roofed with zinc. He sleeps with his grandmom on a local stick bed with grass mattress and thick cloth for covering. The father do small scale farming, he grows rice, cassava and vegetables for their feeding. The wife is a student attending high school in the provincial headquarter town. The combined annual income of US $ 219.00 is not enough to cater for the basic needs of the family. Gibril is in good health and enjoys playing with local toys at home
Country: Sierra Leone

Jeneba

Jeneba is from the Limba ethnic group. She lives in a small village surrounded by rocky hills, farm bushes and vast grassland in the north of the country with her parents and siblings. Her parents are subsistence farmers. They grow their crops mainly for home consumption and sell little to augment their other basic needs. Their annual income of US$ 133 is not adequate to meet the basic needs of the family. Jeneba parents share a single room with all the children and they sleep on a local mattress with poor beddings. Her mother manages to prepare two meals a day. She is a warm and obedient small girl who gets on well with her peers. She is not yet in school. She enjoys playing Local games with her peers. She is in good health
Country: Sierra Leone

Ngorok

Ngorok is a young lovely girl who comes from Turkana County in the Northern Kenya. She lives with her parents, one sister and one brother. They live in a simple, grass-thatched house with mud walls. The family gets water from a nearby river. The water is not safe for drinking. The father is the main breadwinner of the family, who washes other people’s cars to meet the family needs. The family’s annual income is less than US $ 360, which is not enough to cater for family basic needs. The mother approached the project for assistance. Ngorok is not of school- going age and she likes playing with dolls made of local materials. Your sponsorship will be of great help to this lovely girl and the family

Titus

Titus is a friendly young boy who lives in an extended family in northern part of Uganda. He is attending early child hood education and he loves drawing pictures. His ambition is to become a teacher if all goes well and he can now count one up to ten. He moves to school on foot for about 3 kilometres on foot and sometimes if he feels very tired, he does not go. This has made him to be irregular at school and miss some important lessons that could help him grow up well. His father is a peasant farmer who practice small scale farming in order to provide food for the family. This is sometimes so challenging since the harvest is usually poor because of using rudimentary farming method besides serious pests and diseases alongside climatic changes that leads to drought and sometimes heavy down pour that results into floods. This has left Titus with inadequate basic needs that could have helped him to thrive through his childhood very well
Country: Uganda

Jesca

Jesca lives with her parents and siblings in one of the remote villages in Northern Uganda in absolute poverty and she lives in a family where all the siblings and parents share the same grass thatched house made up of mud. Her parents are peasant farmers and as such, they do not have the capacity to provide all the basic needs to the entire family members including Jesca because they cannot even afford to have 1US dollar a day to feed the family members. This condition has made life so difficult for them. Jesca needs to study and become a Nurse but this may not be possible with the condition of her parents. Your desire to sponsor Jesca will be an opportunity not only for her but her entire family
Country: Uganda.
